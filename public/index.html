TL;DR

My name is Dana. I'm a web developer and technology enthusiast living in Denver, Colorado. I get excited about technology that improves lives, products & brands with exceptional design, and companies with audacious goals.

How I Got Here

I was born on a warm spring night in Norwalk, Connecticut...

Twenty years later I stumbled into the world of startups and never looked back. I love working for mission-driven companies with big goals and relentless drive.

My past experiences have ranged from urban-agriculture, to pro-consumer online banking, to on-demand services, to B2B craft coffee. All have been invaluable experiences that have helped shape how I think about business, problem solving, and the direction our society is moving in.
