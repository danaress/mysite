My Work

Check out some of my recent work.

Streak is a web application for easily tracking daily habits.

Problem:

Most habit tracking apps are overloaded with features and options that the average user doesn't need or want. People often stop using them because the logging process is too time-consuming and disruptive.

Solution:

Streak is the easiest way to keep track of your most important daily habits. Each day, you receive a text message at a specified time, asking if you've met your goals for the day. Simply respond with "Y"s or "N"s to log whether or not you've met your goals. You can check your progress simply by logging in.

Design:

The purpose of Streak is to be as easy-to-use and undisruptive as possible. That purpose helped inform the design of the app, leading to a minimal and uncluttered aesthetic. The focus is on showing the user the information that's important to them, as opposed to complex or distracting design elements.

Current Status:

Streak is currently live as a web application. The goal is to release it on the App Store for free.
